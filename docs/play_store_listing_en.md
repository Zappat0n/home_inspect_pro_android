## Full Description

Professional home inspections, simplified.

HomeInspectPro is the all-in-one mobile app for home inspectors. Create detailed, standards-compliant inspection reports directly from your phone or tablet — even without an internet connection.

**Country-Specific Checklists**

Start every inspection with a professional checklist tailored to your region:
- USA: InterNACHI and ASHI standards
- Canada: CAHPI standards
- Spain: ITE and IEE standards

Checklist items are organized by category (roof, plumbing, electrical, HVAC, foundation, and more) so you can work through each section systematically.

**Photo Capture & Offline Queue**

Attach photos to any checklist item directly from your device camera. Photos are queued locally using IndexedDB when you're offline, then automatically uploaded when connectivity is restored. No inspection data is lost — ever.

**Digital Signatures**

Capture your client's signature on-site with native PencilKit support on iPad. Signatures are embedded directly into the final PDF report for a professional, tamper-evident finish.

**Professional PDF Reports**

Generate polished PDF reports with your branding, inspection details, categorized checklist results, photo evidence, defect summaries, and client signature. Reports are generated instantly and delivered to your client's email with one tap.

**Offline Mode**

Inspect anywhere — basements, attics, rural properties — without worrying about cell signal. The entire inspection workflow works offline. Data syncs automatically when you're back online.

**Hotwire Native**

Built with Hotwire Native for a fast, native mobile experience. Access the device camera, signature pad, and file system directly from the app with no browser lag.

**Multi-Language**

Full support for English and Spanish. Switch languages at any time to serve a broader client base.
